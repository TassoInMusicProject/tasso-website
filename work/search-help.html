Search help

For text searches, type any full or partial word to highlight matching words in the score. For music searches, both rhythm and/or pitch can be specified. Each note starts with a rhythm, followed by a pitch (see below). When notes have both rhythm and pitch or pitches only, spaces are optional. Spaces between successive rhythms are required.

Rhythm    0, 1, 2, 4, 8 breve, whole, half, quarter, eighth.
. augmentation dot (e.g., 2. 4)
Pitch a  through  g Diatonic pitch names (any chromatic alteration).
n, #, - Optional chromatic alteration.
r rest
* any single pitch

Example music searches

cdefg Pitches C, D, E, F, and G in sequence with any duration.
1cdefg C whole note followed by D, E, F, and G with any duration.
2 1 2 half note, then whole note, then half note on any pitches.
